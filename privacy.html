Your Privacy Matters

Transparency, trust, and responsible data practices are part of our commitment to you.

Last updated: June 1, 2025

Her Balanced Body ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website located at herbalancedbody.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about visitors to our website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions: Your name, email address, and message when you contact us.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, anonymized information about pages visited, time on site, and referral sources.
  • Cookies: Small data files placed on your device to enhance user experience and support analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and messages

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data for third‑party marketing.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies to support essential website functionality and anonymous traffic analysis. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some features may not function optimally.

4. Third‑Party Services

We may use trusted third‑party providers including:

  • Google Analytics — anonymized traffic insights (Privacy Policy)
  • Pinterest — external profile links (Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify — secure website hosting (Privacy Policy)

5. Affiliate Links & Advertising

Some content may include affiliate links. If you purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products aligned with our values. All affiliate relationships comply with FTC guidelines.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our content is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of personal information. To make a request, please use our contact page.

8. Data Retention

We retain submitted information only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries. After that, data is deleted or anonymized.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any updates.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us here.
