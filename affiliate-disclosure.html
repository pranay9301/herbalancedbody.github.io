We believe in transparency about how this website may be supported.

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Her Balanced Body may participate in affiliate marketing programs. This means that some links on this website may be affiliate links, and we may earn a small commission if you click through and make a purchase, at no extra cost to you.

An affiliate link is a special tracking link. If you click one of these links and purchase a product or service, we may receive compensation from the company or platform involved. Your purchase price generally remains the same.

2. Why Affiliate Links May Be Used

Affiliate links may be used to recommend products, tools, resources, or services that are relevant to the topics discussed on Her Balanced Body, such as fitness equipment, wellness tools, walking accessories, supplements, or other related items aligned with our content.

3. Editorial Integrity

We aim to maintain editorial integrity in all content. Any product, tool, or service mentioned on Her Balanced Body is intended to be selected based on relevance, usefulness, and alignment with our audience and values. Affiliate relationships do not guarantee positive coverage and are not intended to influence the honesty of our editorial content.

4. FTC and Transparency Compliance

We intend to comply with applicable advertising and disclosure guidelines, including Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidance where relevant. When affiliate links are used in content, we aim to provide clear disclosure so readers understand that a commission may be earned.

5. No Extra Cost to You

If you make a purchase through an affiliate link on this website, there is typically no additional cost to you. Any commission earned helps support the continued creation, maintenance, and improvement of Her Balanced Body.

6. Your Responsibility

Please use your own judgment before purchasing any product or service mentioned on this website. Always review product details, policies, ingredients, safety information, and suitability for your personal needs. Nothing on this website should be interpreted as a guarantee of results.

7. Future Monetization

Her Balanced Body may also use other forms of monetization in the future, including display advertising, sponsored partnerships, digital products, or email-based recommendations. Where relevant, we aim to disclose such relationships appropriately.
