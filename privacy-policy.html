Your Privacy Matters

Transparency, trust, and responsible data practices are part of our commitment to you.

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Her Balanced Body ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at https://herbalancedbody.github.io/. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and manage information about visitors to our website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions: If you contact us, we may collect the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and message.
  • Analytics data: We may collect aggregated and anonymized information such as pages visited, time on site, referral sources, browser type, device type, and general geographic region through analytics tools.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: Small data files may be stored on your device to support analytics, website functionality, and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use information collected through this website to:

  • Respond to inquiries and messages
  • Improve website content, structure, and performance
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Maintain website security, functionality, and technical performance

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data for third-party marketing purposes.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to support essential website functionality and anonymous traffic analysis. Cookies can help us understand how visitors interact with our site and improve the user experience over time. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer; however, some features may not function optimally.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers and services, including:

  • Google Analytics — to understand website traffic and visitor behavior in an aggregated and anonymized way (Privacy Policy)
  • Pinterest — for external profile, content discovery, and traffic sharing purposes (Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting and delivery of this static website (Privacy Statement)

These third-party services may collect or process data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies directly.

Some content on Her Balanced Body may include affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. We only aim to recommend products, tools, or resources that are relevant to our content and audience. All affiliate relationships are intended to comply with applicable disclosure standards, including FTC guidelines.

This website may also include advertising or monetization features in the future. If ads are added later, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

6. Email Communications

If email subscriptions, newsletters, or downloadable resources are offered in the future and you voluntarily subscribe, we may collect your email address and related signup information for the purpose described at the time of signup. You may unsubscribe from email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link provided in those emails.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our content is intended for adults and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information from a child has been collected in error, we will take reasonable steps to remove it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access, correction, or deletion of information you have submitted. To make a request, please use our Contact Page.

9. Data Retention

We retain submitted information only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, maintain records where appropriate, or support legitimate website operations. After that, information may be deleted, anonymized, or no longer actively used.

10. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through this website. However, no method of internet transmission, storage, or electronic processing is guaranteed to be completely secure. By using this site, you acknowledge this inherent risk.

11. External Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of external websites. Please review their privacy policies separately before providing personal information.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in website practices, legal requirements, or future services. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of those updates.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us here.
