Why Weight Loss Can Feel Different After 40

For many women, weight loss after 40 can feel more complicated than it did in earlier years. Changing hormones, shifting metabolism, muscle loss, stress, sleep changes, recovery needs, and perimenopause-related patterns may all influence how the body responds.

This category is designed to help you better understand those changes without relying on shame, fear, or extreme solutions. The goal is to offer calm, practical guidance that supports sustainable progress through realistic habits, strength support, balanced meals, and a more informed approach to wellness.
