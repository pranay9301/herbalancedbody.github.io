Understanding Belly Fat Through a Wider Lens

Belly fat can feel especially frustrating when you are eating well, trying to stay consistent, and still not seeing the changes you expect. For many women, stress, sleep, hormones, recovery, age-related shifts, and metabolic patterns may all play a role in how weight is stored and how progress feels.

This category is designed to help you explore those connections in a calm, beginner-friendly way. Rather than relying on fear-based wellness advice, these articles focus on education, sustainable support, and a more realistic understanding of hormone-aware weight loss.
